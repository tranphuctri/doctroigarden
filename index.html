Our Story

Every Flavor Tells a Story

Dốc Trời Garden, formly known as Dốc Trời Pond, was established in 2015 with the purpose of making a relaxing place for tourists that want to have such the wildest and the most unique experience in Da Lat City. With many years of learning and experiencing food cuisine, we finally have large amount of loyal guests. Now, to serve all our beloved guests better, we finally decided to open Dốc Trời Garden to help tourists reach easier rather than before and still deliver that special and explosive tastes in every delicacy.
